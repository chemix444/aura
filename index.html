Our Story

Redefining nightlife and event hosting in the heart of Naperville

Rising from the legacy of the iconic Matrix Club, Aura Banquet and Lounge brings a fresh, elevated experience to Naperville's entertainment scene. We've reimagined the space to deliver a sophisticated atmosphere where luxury meets energy.

Whether you're celebrating life's milestones at a private banquet, enjoying craft cocktails in our lounge, or dancing the night away, Aura is designed to make every visit unforgettable. Our state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems, curated menus, and attentive service set a new standard for the western suburbs.

We believe Naperville deserves a world-class venue — and that's exactly what we've built.
