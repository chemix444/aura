Our Story

Redefining nightlife and event hosting in the heart of Naperville

Aura Club and Conventions brings a fresh, elevated experience to Naperville's entertainment scene. Designed from the ground up, our space delivers a sophisticated atmosphere where luxury meets energy.

Whether you're celebrating life's milestones at a private event, enjoying craft cocktails in our club, or dancing the night away, Aura is designed to make every visit unforgettable. Our state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems, curated menus, and attentive service set a new standard for the western suburbs.

We believe Naperville deserves a world-class venue — and that's exactly what we've built.
